🌿 Quick Wellness Tips

  • Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep and keep a consistent schedule.
  • A 20-minute walk can lift your mood and reduce cortisol.
  • Try 4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4 sec, hold 7, exhale 8.
  • Writing about feelings for 10 min/day reduces tension over time.
  • Stay hydrated — dehydration worsens anxiety and fatigue.

Feeling stressed? Try this

The 4-7-8 breathing technique calms your nervous system in minutes.

Press Start

Cycle: 0 / 3

How it works

4 – 7 – 8 Technique

  • 4

    Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds. Let your chest and belly expand fully.

  • 7

    Hold your breath for 7 seconds. Keep your body still and your mind focused.

  • 8

    Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds. Release all tension with the breath.

  • 3

    Repeat 3 cycles for the best effect. You should feel calmer within one minute.
