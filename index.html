Why MindSpace?

Built for students,
by students

Created at Mbarara University of Science and Technology by IT students who understand academic pressure first-hand.

  • 100% free and completely confidential
  • Designed for university students in Uganda
  • Evidence-based coping strategies and tools
  • Anonymous peer support network
  • Instant access to emergency contacts
  • Works on any device, no installation needed

Recommended Reading

Carefully selected books to help you understand and manage your mental wellness.

📘

The Anxiety and Worry Workbook

Clark & Beck

Practical CBT techniques to manage anxiety and overthinking.

📗

The Body Keeps the Score

Bessel van der Kolk

Explores how trauma reshapes the body and mind, and how healing begins.

📙

Feeling Good: The New Mood Therapy

David D. Burns

A self-help classic on overcoming depression and negative thinking.

📕

Maybe You Should Talk to Someone

Lori Gottlieb

A therapist goes to therapy herself — warm, funny, and deeply insightful.

📓

Lost Connections

Johann Hari

Explores the real causes of depression beyond brain chemistry.
