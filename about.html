About MindSpace

A student-built platform dedicated to making mental health support accessible to every university student in Uganda.

Our Mission

Making student wellbeing
a priority

At Mbarara University of Science and Technology, we saw fellow students struggling silently — with exam pressure, loneliness, financial stress, and academic expectations that felt impossible to meet.

MindSpace was born from that observation. Our goal is to build a safe, accessible digital space where any student can check in with their emotions, find support, and know they are not alone.

We believe that mental health is just as important as academic performance — and that every student deserves a place to breathe.

How It Works

Getting started is simple

Step 01
📱

Visit the Platform

Open MindSpace on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No installation, no signup required.

Step 02
😊

Check In With Your Mood

Log how you're feeling using the Mood Tracker. Takes under a minute and builds over time.

Step 03
📖

Write in Your Journal

Open your private journal and write freely. Guided prompts are available if you need a start.

Step 04
🤝

Connect with a counselor or access emergency contacts instantly through the Contact page.

Key Facts

What every student
should know

  • Mental health challenges are more common than academic failure — yet far less discussed on campus.
  • Stigma remains the biggest barrier to students seeking support at universities in Uganda.
  • Regular journaling and mood tracking have been proven to reduce anxiety levels significantly.
  • Community and peer connection are among the strongest predictors of mental wellness outcomes.
  • Early digital self-care interventions can prevent serious mental health crises from developing.
